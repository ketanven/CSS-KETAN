GLS UNIVERSITY

GLS University, formerly known as Gujarat Law Society, is a prominent educational institution located in the vibrant city of Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. Established in 1927, GLS University has a rich history of providing quality education and fostering academic excellence in a wide range of disciplines. The institution has evolved over the years and expanded its academic offerings to cater to the diverse educational needs of students. GLS University is known for its commitment to holistic development, innovation, and a student-centric approach to learning. It offers a variety of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in fields such as law, management, commerce, arts, science, education, and more.

GLS University is established in the year of 2015 with the objective of providing an ideal and creative learning environment and continuing the tradition of excellence in education of the sponsoring body of the University, viz., Gujarat Law Society (GLS) Gujarat Law Society, established in the year 1927, is one of the largest and oldest educational institutions in the State of Gujarat. GLS was promoted by luminaries SardarVallabhbhai Patel, Shri Ganesh Mavlankar, the first Speaker of the country and Shri I.M. Nanavati with the vision of Excellence in Education.
